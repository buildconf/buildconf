Schedule

Monday

  • UX Book Club Belfast

    6.00pm

    Design By Front

    Free

    UX Book Club Belfast has kindly offered to open its doors to any Build attendees interested in discussing this months title, Alan Cooper's The Inmates are Running the Asylum. Hosted at the offices of Design By Front, beer & pizza will accompany the always lively discussion.

  • 'Inspired Interfaces' Exhibition

    7.30pm

    University of Ulster

    Free

    Inspired by the groundbreaking work of celebrated industrial designer Dieter Rams, ‘Inspired Interfaces’ samples details of Rams’ most illustrious interfaces, painstakingly recreated in nothing but HTML5 & CSS3.

  • 'Illustrative Initials' Exhibition

    7.30pm

    University of Ulster

    Free

    Accompanying Ms. Hische’s lecture later in the week, we’re proud to be hosting ‘Illustrative Initials’, an exhibition of letter-pressed drop caps from Jessica’s long-running Daily Drop Cap project. Letterpressed prints from a selection from the first six alphabets will also be on sale.

Tuesday

  • Workshops

    9am - 5pm

    The Merchant Hotel

    Register Now

    We've got a host of single full-day workshops from Liz Danzico, Frank Chimero, Tim Brown and the Web Standardistas on user interviews, delighting users, typesetting on the web and paper-prototyping. Breakfast, lunch and regular refreshments will be provided. Please note that a workshop pass is required to attend.

  • An Evening With Jessica Hische

    6.30pm

    University of Ulster

    £10/£8 (free for pass-holders)

    Brooklyn-based typographer and illustrator Jessica Hische hosts a lecture on her portfolio of work — including her award-winning hand-illustrated type, long-running Daily Drop Cap project and the measurable benefits of all-night X-Files marathons.

  • The Standardistas Open Book Exam

    9:00pm

    21 Social

    £5 (free for pass-holders) - Register Now

    Featuring ten rounds on everything from the history of 68K Assembler to the experiments of Dr. Lawrence Angelo, this pub quiz with a difference requires a stealthy level of cheating to be in with a chance of winning one of the dozens of prizes on offer — including a brand new iPad!

Thursday

  • fr00tball

    12pm

    Queen's PEC

    Donations Welcome - Register Now

    Organised in aid of charity by a group of Build attendees, fr00tball is essentially a football tournament for geeks. Suggested as the perfect way to sweat off your hangover, everyone is welcome to turn up and take part or lend support. Donations welcome.

  • An Evening With Threadless

    6.30pm

    University of Ulster

    £10/£8 (free for pass-holders)

    Threadless User Experience Director Brock Rumer will be hosting a lecture on the story of Chicago-based online apparel store Threadless — discussing everything from tees, collaborations, mistakes, successes and working with artists all over the world.

  • Onotate Launch Party

    9pm

    21 Social

    Free

    Local development studio Rumble Labs will be hosting a party to celebrate the public launch of their web app, Onotate - which enables designers and developers to present and discuss mockups with clients and team members. Expect beers, nibbles and a great deal of craic.

Friday

    Queen's Film Theatre

    £5 (free for pass-holders)

And some dinosaurs. And David Attenborough's brother. you know, the one who was Santa in that other thing? But yeah. Dinosaurs.
