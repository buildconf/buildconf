Ethos

 

The Manual will help smart designers, like you, look at your next project in a different way, questioning your choices.

 
 

We hear the voices of a conversation moving through the community of the craftspeople of interactive design: popping up at conferences, in tweets and blog posts, and in adrenalized chats over beer when we gather. The Manual chronicles these voices in a beautiful print artifact. It archives the insights of our best thinkers and writers—both the well-known and the quietly working—as we define a more mature understanding of the discipline of web design and more effectively tell the story of our work.

Web design is coming into its own as a discipline, with its roots firmly in graphic design, yet now creating its own principles, tools, and visual language. Everything we do requires a good hard look. Our community has rightly focused on the "how" of design on the web. But you won’t find markup and code, tutorials, and galleries on these pages. The Manual looks at the "why": the "why" of our designs, the "why" of the transformative power of our work, and the "why" of where we're heading.

 
 

Walk away from your work.

Where do we find inspiration? Not in the distracting devices we peck at with fingers and thumbs. Often, it comes not from technology but through reflection or experiences.

Read The Manual at the park, by the river, in a pub, over coffee. It’s crafted to the standards that designers expect—its beauty is textured, tangible. Each article is paired with a delightful illustration. Each page is a revelation. After savoring what’s inside, you’ll return to your work invigorated, musing, challenged, or on fire. You’ll want more than ever to be part of this story.
