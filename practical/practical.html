Practical

Wednesday at The Waterfront Studio

Discuss ideas or make things with others, catch up on work, or just recover from your hangover—plus, there’s coffee and food all day. You’re welcome.

Group Discussions

Build attracts some of design’s best and brightest. Get together in small groups and lead, listen, and participate in conversations ranging from what you’re working on to problems you’re experiencing and ideas you have.

Work With Others

Last year, many attendees desired a chance to work with others they met at the workshops, pubs, and during general chit-chat. Take some time to further your efforts in small groups and build something wonderful.

Work on Your Own

Need to check in on client work or the office? Take a breather from the excitement and cross off a few to-do items from our wifi connection while enjoying the food and coffee.

Play

Here for the week just to enjoy yourself? We’ve got Lego™, wifi, good company, coffee, and food. Come down and enjoy the day.
