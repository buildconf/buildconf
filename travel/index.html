Dining

Victoria Square

Home to Wagamama, Gourmet Burger Kitchen, Pizza Express, Nando’s and more.

Little Wing

Great new pizza joint, serving up traditionally made Naples style pizzas.

The Windmill

Greasy spoon on Castle Lane, perfect hangover faire, does an excellent Ulster Fry.

Drinking

The John Hewitt

Best selection of draught beers in town, including some choice local lagers, blondes & ales.

Duke of York

The typical pub of choice for Belfast’s geeks, does a great pint of Guinness.

The Garrick

Comprehensive selection of bottled beers from around the world, free wifi too!

Coffee

Starbucks

Located on the edge of Victoria Square, standard Starbucks-grade coffee & free wifi.

Ground

Just above Waterstones on Fountain Street, free wifi & great selection of buns.

Deanes Deli

On Bedford Street, probably the best coffee in town and great for lunch, no wifi.
