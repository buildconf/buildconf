The Sequel

Issue 02

The articles focus on bringing a greater depth and maturity to our craft and profession, with contributions in the first issue from wonderfully talented artists and writers such as Simon Collison, Frank Chimero, Liz Danzico, Dan Rubin, Jon Tan, and the Web Standardistas.

The journal features a beautiful foil-stamped design on the cover. It’s not a magazine—it’s a journal, a quarterly of heft, weight and gravitas that matches the content within. It measures 6 x 9 inches, is hardbound with 50# white text sheet and a 100# cover stock.
