‘An Evening With’ Lectures

Our ‘An Evening With’ lecture series makes a return to the programme of fringe events this year, including talks from renowned typographer, designer and founder of FontShop Erik Spiekermann on Tuesday 8th November—and cartoonist, comic theorist and author of ‘Understanding Comics’ Scott McCloud on Wednesday 9th November. Entry to the lectures is included with a conference and/or workshop pass.
